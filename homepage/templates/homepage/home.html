Some changes have been made!

The site has been updated to support automatically finding Ansiblex Jump Gates through ESI. This requires additional scopes, so you will notice when you log in that we request the following scopes now:

  • esi-location.read_location.v1
  • esi-search.search_structures.v1 (new)
  • esi-universe.read_structures.v1 (new)
  • esi-ui.write_waypoint.v1
These two additional scopes allow the website to search through your alliance's jump gates to automatically keep the master list up to date. Adding these scopes is completely optional, but highly encouraged. If you have any questions about the information we will be searching for, don't hesitate to check out the source or contact me through our Discord.
